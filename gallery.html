Some of the cool places I've been to

Langtang National Park

My fried Latane and I went on a solo backpacking trek to Langtang National Park in the summer of 2018. It was my first ever time trekking without my father and I finally learnt how to read the trail maps and bargain with local lodge owners.


Palo Duro Canyon State Park

My girlfriend and I roadtripped up to Colorado and stopped by Palo Duro Canyon State Park in the summer of 2020. It was my first ever time seeing a canyon and I disntinctly remember being in awe.


View from Parthali

A beautiful view from a vista about 3 hours away from Kathmandu. My father and I made weekly trips to areas around the Kathmandu valley and were fortunate to see the Annapurna range in all it's beauty.



Sky Pond - Rocky Mountain National Park

Perhaps the most beautiful place in America that I've been to. The trail was hard and tricky but the payoff was incredible.

Narayanthan View Point

Perhaps the most memorable place I've ever been to. After a short and steep hike from where you can park, you end up above the clouds and can see all the way down to the souther flats. Incredbile
